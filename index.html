About Us

Welcome to Dice and Dealers , a haven for gamers and a community built on the roll of a die and the flip of a card. Formed in 2025, our club is a safe and comfortable space where everyone—from the youngest aspiring hero to the most seasoned veteran—can find a place at our table.

We meet every Tuesday from 5:30 PM to 10:00 PM at The Embankment, 282-284 Arkwright St, Nottingham NG2 2GR , and every Wednesday from 6:30 PM to 10:00 PM at The Oxford Bulwell, Highbury Rd, Nottingham NG6 9BU . Whether you're looking to lead a regiment into battle, embark on a quest to save the realm, or simply enjoy a quiet evening with friends, our doors are always open. We believe in the magic of storytelling, the thrill of competition, and the unbreakable bonds forged over a shared love for the hobby.
